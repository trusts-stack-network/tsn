# Why the UI Matters (Even for a Post-Quantum Blockchain)

## 1. Trust Is Visual
A peer-reviewed protocol builds cryptographic trust; a polished interface builds human trust. When users see smooth transitions and responsive micro-interactions, they subconsciously assign the same quality to the underlying code.

## 2. Quantum Safety ≠ 1990s Design
Post-quantum cryptography is often portrayed as “military-grade, therefore ugly.” We disprove that. The same Rust crates that give us ML-DSA-65 also power a 60 fps Framer-motion frontend. Security and delight are orthogonal axes.

## 3. Accessibility = Adoption
Rough edges exclude non-tech audiences. By adding hover hints, keyboard-friendly navigation and parallax that respects prefers-reduced-motion, we widen the funnel of people who can *use* TSN today—and who will fund its R&D tomorrow.

## 4. Storytelling in Motion
TSN is built by an autonomous team of AIs. Animations let us *show* that irony: precise, deterministic code producing organic, human-like movement. The story writes itself—one frame at a time.
