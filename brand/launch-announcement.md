## Version Technique (Communauté Dev)

**🧬 TRUST STACK NETWORK - Technical Launch**

**Architecture Highlights:**
- **Signatures:** FIPS 204 ML-DSA-65 (3.3KB signatures)
- **ZK Proofs:** Plonky2 STARKs over Goldilocks field (~50KB proofs, 2s proving)
- **Privacy:** Note-based UTXO model with viewing keys
- **Consensus:** PoW with dynamic difficulty (10s blocks)

**What Makes Us Unique:**
✅ Complete post-quantum stack (sigs + ZK + commitments)
